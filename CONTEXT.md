# 下班复仇记 — Domain Glossary

## Core Concepts

- **Run (逃脱)** — one full playthrough, 5 days + optional endless mode
- **Day (天)** — one in-game day containing ~10 timed events, ending with a Boss fight
- **Turn (回合)** — one player turn in combat: play cards, then end turn for enemy action
- **Floor (层)** — one endless-mode fight; floor counter increments after each win
- **Breakdown (崩溃)** — when stress reaches 10; lose one turn, take 30% max HP damage, stress resets to 5
- **Route (路线)** — branching path choice; each day offers 2-3 routes with different risk/reward profiles

## Cards (卡牌)

- **Faction (派系)** — every card belongs to one: 通用, 卷王, 摸鱼, 养生, 社交
- **Synergy (共鸣)** — playing 3+ same-faction cards in one turn triggers bonus effects
- **Rarity (稀有度)** — C (白/Common), U (蓝/Uncommon), R (金/Rare)
- **Card Types**:
  - **Attack (攻击)** — deals damage to enemy
  - **Skill (技能)** — buffs, shields, healing, utility
  - **Spell (法术)** — powerful one-shot; removed from deck after use (does not enter discard)
  - **Equipment (装备)** — persistent effect while active; replaced when new equipment played
  - **Curse (诅咒)** — negative card forced into deck by enemies/events; wastes a draw slot

## Relics (遗物)

- Passive items granting permanent bonuses for the run
- **Rarity tiers**: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Legendary
- **Cursed Relics** — powerful effect with a permanent drawback
- **Set Bonuses** — matching relics from same theme grant extra effects

## Stress (压力)

- 0-3: no effect
- 4-6: +25% damage dealt
- 7-9: +50% damage dealt, +1 card drawn per turn
- 10: Breakdown — skip turn, lose 30% max HP, reset to 5
- Stress decays by half (floor) at end of each day; does not fully reset

## Enemies (敌人)

- **Regular (普通)** — basic enemies from day-tiered pools
- **Elite (精英)** — tougher enemies with better rewards
- **Boss** — day-end boss fight; only Boss wins offer relic rewards
- **Super Boss** — endless mode every 10 floors; scaled boss with extra HP
- Enemies have factions matching card factions; same-faction enemies gain synergy bonuses

## Routes (路线)

- Each day presents 2-3 event routes after the morning transition
- **Safe route** — easier fights, guaranteed healing, fewer rewards
- **Risk route** — elite fights, curse cards, rare relics/cards
- **Neutral route** — balanced events and rewards
